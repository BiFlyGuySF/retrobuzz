15 ’90s Toys You Forgot Existed (#7 Will Hit You Right in the Feels)

RetroBuzz Team2025-08-29
Ad slot (336×280)

From Gooey alien eggs to the chaos of Pogs—how many do you remember?

  1. Tamagotchi Your first taste of responsibility…
  2. Furby. It watched. It learned. It spoke in tongues. Still kinda cute?
  3. Talkboy. Thanks, *Home Alone 2*. We all became tiny audio engineers overnight.
  4. Skip-It. The more you played, the more your ankle suffered. Peak fun.
  5. Gak. Nickelodeon in your hands. The smell? Unforgettable, for better or worse.
  6. Polly Pocket. Tiny worlds, massive imagination. And a choking hazard buffet.
  7. Bop It. Twist it! Pull it! Panic under pressure like a champion.
  8. Moon Shoes. “Mini-trampolines for your feet” was exactly as chaotic as it sounds.
  9. Yomega Yo-Yo. Brain, Fireball… tricks for days, tangles for weeks.
  10. Creepy Crawlers. Bake your own bugs. Probably not FDA-approved fun.
  11. Stretch Armstrong. Gym goals since 1976, but the 90s made him a superstar again.
  12. Pogs & Slammers. Playground economics and the great lunch-money recession of ’95.
  13. HitClips. One chorus on repeat—proof we’ll tolerate anything to feel cool.
  14. Laser Challenge. DIY laser tag before it was sleek and accurate.
  15. Super Soaker CPS 2000. Armed and extremely hydrated.
